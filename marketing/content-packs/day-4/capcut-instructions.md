# CapCut Edit Instructies — Day 4

## Script 1 (09:00)

**Duration:** 25-30 seconds
**Format:** 9:16 vertical
**Thumbnail text:** "RHYTHM > WILLPOWER"

### Stappen:
1. Importeer je gefilmde video
2. Ga naar "Captions" → "Auto Captions" → selecteer taal
3. OF importeer `subtitles-1.srt` handmatig
4. Style: Bold, wit, donkere achtergrond, gecentreerd onderaan
5. Font grootte: 32-36pt

### On-screen text overlays:
- [0-2s] "High performers don't have more willpower. They have better rhythms."
- [18-25s] "Move your most important work to the first 3 hours of your day."
- [25-30s] "Link in bio"

### Export settings:
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 (9:16)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Quality: High
- Format: MP4
